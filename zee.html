Why Reading Matters Early

Every page turn builds a future.

As a speech-language pathologist, Sarah knows that the seeds of communication are planted long before a child speaks their first word. Here's why early reading is one of the most powerful gifts you can give.

Bonding

Reading together creates moments of closeness — your voice, your warmth, your attention. These shared rituals shape a child's sense of safety and connection.

Language Development

Hearing words, rhythms, and stories from infancy builds the vocabulary and listening skills children draw from when they begin to speak — and later, to read.

Imagination

Stories invite children into worlds beyond their own — building empathy, creativity, and the curiosity that fuels lifelong learning. It all starts with one book.
