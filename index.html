Meet Your Therapist

Sarah Bipath

M.S., CCC-SLP, CBS — Founder & Speech-Language Pathologist

Sarah is a board-certified speech-language pathologist with years of experience helping clients of all ages — from infants to seniors — find their voice. Originally from New York City and now based in Central Florida, she brings expertise across schools, hospitals, early intervention, and private practice.

Her approach is deeply personal: every client is treated as a whole person, not a diagnosis, with care tailored to their unique goals and lifestyle.
