Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Effective: April 28, 2025 · Last updated: April 28, 2025

Sarah's Tactically Aligned Speech Know How's LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit www.sarahstask.com.

Information we collect

We may collect personal information, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (when submitted through contact forms).
  • Payment information (when purchasing services).
  • IP address, browser type, and device data (for website analytics).

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you (e.g., appointment reminders and responses to inquiries).
  • To process transactions securely.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., payment processors and scheduling software).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
