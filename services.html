Specialty Services

Specialized care for complex needs.

Beyond traditional speech therapy, Sarah brings advanced training in three specialty areas — each addressing root causes that conventional treatment alone may not reach.

Orofacial Myofunctional Therapy

OMT addresses the root causes of persistent speech, feeding, and breathing challenges through targeted exercises and behavioral modifications that promote proper coordination of the oral musculature — often producing lasting change where surface-level treatment falls short.

Craniosacral Fascial Therapy

CFT is the work of Dr. Barry Gillespie. The Gillespie Approach gently unwinds and releases accumulated fascial strain, allowing for optimal mobility and improved brain function — enhancing responsiveness to therapeutic intervention targeting oral structures and nervous system regulation.

Lactation Services

Achieve breastfeeding goals throughout your lactation journey with expert support on optimizing feeding positions, milk expression, and feeding duration — combining clinical expertise with the warmth and patience new families need.
