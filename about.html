About Sarah

Sarah Bipath

M.S., CCC-SLP, CBS — Founder & Speech-Language Pathologist

Sarah is a board-certified speech-language pathologist originally from New York City, now based in Central Florida. She's spent years working with clients of all ages — from infants to seniors — across nearly every clinical setting imaginable.

Her experience spans public and private schools, home health, early intervention, skilled nursing facilities, hospitals, outpatient clinics, private practices, and virtual platforms. That breadth means whatever you or your loved one is navigating, Sarah has likely seen it before — and knows how to help.

Beyond her SLP credentials, Sarah is also a Certified Breastfeeding Specialist, a trained Craniosacral Fascial Therapist, and an Orofacial Myologist specializing in the treatment of Orofacial Myofunctional Disorders across the lifespan.

She's passionate about collaborating with family members, caregivers, and other professionals to deliver the best quality of care — and she stays current with the latest research and best practices in speech therapy.

I treat every client as a whole person — not a diagnosis. Care should feel like a partnership, built around your goals, your lifestyle, and your story.

— Sarah Bipath, M.S., CCC-SLP, CBS

Certifications & Training

Credentials that back the care.

Sarah maintains advanced certifications across multiple specialties — so the treatment you receive is rooted in proven, evidence-based practice.

CCC-SLP

ASHA Certified Speech-Language Pathologist

The gold standard credential from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association, requiring graduate education, supervised clinical experience, and ongoing professional development.

CBS

Certified Breastfeeding Specialist

Specialized training in lactation support, including positioning, milk expression, feeding duration, and addressing breastfeeding challenges across the lactation journey.

PROMPT

PROMPT Trained

Tactile-kinesthetic approach for treating speech sound disorders, using touch cues to support muscle movement and articulation.

CFT

Craniosacral Fascial Therapist

Trained in The Gillespie Approach to gently unwind accumulated fascial strain, supporting optimal mobility and nervous system regulation.

OMT

Orofacial Myologist

Specialized in treating Orofacial Myofunctional Disorders (OMDs) — addressing root causes of persistent speech, feeding, and breathing challenges.

TOTS

TOTS Training

Tethered Oral Tissues training — assessment and treatment of tongue, lip, and buccal ties affecting feeding, speech, and breathing.
